Code of Conduct & Regulations

Players • Managers • Coaches

12.1 Player Uniform

All players must adhere to the standard kit requirements to be eligible for play.

Apparel: Shorts & T-Shirt
Footwear: Mat Shoes (strictly no metal parts)
Lettering: Numbers on Front & Back in contrast color
Dimensions: 10cm Length x 2cm Width

※ Duplicate numbers within a team are strictly prohibited.

12.2 / 12.3 Rights & Conduct

General Requirement: Every participant must know the rules of the game.

Communication: Only the Coach or Captain may address the Referee.

Prohibited Acts (Punishable):

  • Directly addressing officials without authorization.
  • Making derogatory remarks or gestures.
  • Attempting to influence officials' decisions.
  • Leaving the play field without informing the referee during a match.

12.4 Penalty System

Minor Offence:
Initial verbal warning by the Referee.
Blue / Yellow Card: Issued for repetition of minor offences.
Red Card: Issued for serious offences. Immediate disqualification from the match.

Suspension Rules:

Occurrence Penalty Outcome
2 Yellow Cards (Same Match) Forbidden from current & next match.
Yellow Card (2 Different Matches) Forbidden from the next match.
Red Card (Any Match) Forbidden from current & next match.
