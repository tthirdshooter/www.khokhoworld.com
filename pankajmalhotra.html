Pankaj Malhotra

International Gold Medalist | Global Ambassador

A prominent Kho Kho figure from Jammu & Kashmir, recognized globally for his athletic excellence and administrative leadership.

Quick Profile

  • Hometown: Sainik Colony, Jammu, J&K
  • Current Role: Treasurer, J&K Kho-Kho Association (JKKKA)
  • Expertise: International Technical Expert
  • Ranking: Top 5 Kho Kho Players in India (by major outlets)

Career Highlights & Achievements

International Glory Gold

Represented Team India in the 2018 India-Nepal 5-Match Test Series (5-0 Sweep).

State Leadership

Long-serving Captain of the Jammu & Kashmir State Kho Kho Team.

Ultimate Kho-Kho (UKK)
