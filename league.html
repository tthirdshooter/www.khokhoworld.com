Ultimate Kho Kho (UKK)

Launched in 2022, **Ultimate Kho Kho** revolutionized the sport with a franchise-based model. It introduced the "Wazir," "Powerplay," and "Dream Run" to make the game faster and television-friendly.

  • ✅ Format: 7 players on field (instead of 9)
  • ✅ The Wazir: A specialized attacker who can move in any direction
  • ✅ Points: 2 points per tag, plus bonus points for 'Dream Runs'
  • ✅ Duration: 7-minute turns (standard is 9 mins)
