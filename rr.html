KHO-KHO ARENA

The Toss

No captain shall touch or pick up the coin. If they do, the Referee declares the opponent the winner of the toss immediately.

Decision

After choosing Defense or Attack, the captain cannot change his decision. Decision is final.

Attacker Positioning

Adjacent attackers must face opposite side lines. They cannot leave the block or change face without a KHO.

The Lane

An Attacker cannot cross the Central Lane. A foul occurs if the foot crosses both lines completely.

KHO Execution

Touching the sitter and shouting "KHO" must be simultaneous. Shouting before touching is a FOUL.

Direction

An attacker must follow the shoulder line. Turning more than 90° from the original direction is a Receding Foul.

Penalty: Blue Card

1-Minute Suspension for unnecessary standing or interchanging blocks. Replacement must enter immediately.

Penalty: Red Card

If a team has no substitutes and receives a Red Card or injury, the turn is closed immediately.

Misbehaviour

Pushing, pulling uniform, or hitting the defender is MISBEHAVIOUR and will be penalized.

Defense

A defender is OUT if touched legally, or if they step out of the field. Touching a sitter results in a warning.
